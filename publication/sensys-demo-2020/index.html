A Privacy-Enabled Platform for COVID-19 Applications

Michael August, Christopher Davison, Mamadou H. Diallo, Dhrubajyoti Ghosh, Peeyush Gupta, Christopher T. Graves, Shanshan Han, Michael Holstrom, Pramod Khargonekar, Megan Kline, Sharad Mehrotra, Shantanu Sharma, Nalini Venkatasubramanian, Guoxi Wang, Roberto Yus
November 2020
PDF Cite Slides

Abstract

We present our experiences in adapting and deploying TIPPERS, a novel privacy-enabled IoT data collection and management system for smart spaces, to facilitate the monitoring of adherence to COVID-19 regulations in a university campus and a military facility.

18th ACM Conference on Embedded Networked Sensor Systems (SenSys 2020)
